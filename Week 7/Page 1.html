Little Story

I recently went backpacking with my friends. When we got to our campsite, however, we were bombarded by endless hoardes of goats.
Apparantly, campers have gotten in a bad habbit of feeding all of the goats and so the goats are a little too comfortable with people.
In fact, they were so comfortable with people that they wouldn't even run away from us when we tried to yell them away.
What we had to end up doing was tying a plastic bag to a stick and violently shake it while charging at them!
